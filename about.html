We exist because agile forgot the humans.

Agile Fragile is building the Human Agile category. Not another training institute. A model, a community, and a way to end violent transformations.

Our belief

Humans are the most valuable asset in the organization. Humans make the organization.

What we saw

Agile rolled out on timelines. People pushed, crushed, and blamed when they resisted. Dashboards became judgment. Ceremony became theater. The humans were left out of the transformation that was supposed to be about them.

What we built

Human Agile: a human-centric model to adapt, implement, and scale agile with people at the center. The Human Agile Index to measure where you are. A community for leaders who refuse to break their people.

What we are not

A certification factory. A generic coaching shop. An implementation partner that ships process and leaves. We humanize agile. We help organizations subtract harm and build with their people.
