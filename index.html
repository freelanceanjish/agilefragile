Human Agile Manifesto

Eight principles. One belief: humans make the organization.

Humans are the asset Not the obstacle. Not the rollout risk.
Change with, not to No transformation decided without the people inside it.
Subtract before you add Remove harm before piling on more process.
Trust over tracking Metrics should help teams learn, not fear.
No violent transformation Speed without humanity breaks delivery.
Adapt before you scale Context and people come before playbook.
Leaders enable Not perform agile. Not become human dashboards.
Agile serves the org People do not serve agile.
