Field proof

Enterprise scale, human cost named

Work across federated operating models, multi-country programmes, and regulated industries.

Financial services

Velocity-driven rollout was burning out delivery teams

Leadership saw green dashboards. Teams carried sprints on top of unfinished work. Retros became tracking exercises, not learning.

Outcome: Reset ceremony load, rebuilt psychological safety with leaders in the room, teams reclaimed focus for real delivery.

Telecom

Agile adoption became surveillance, not support

Two-hour training, then weekly velocity judgment. People who raised concerns in retros were managed out within months.

Outcome: Shifted dashboards from judgment to learning signals. Restored agency for teams to reject ceremony that did not help.

Retail & technology

Transformation looked successful on slides, fragile in the building

Product and delivery focus pushed humans to the edge of the design. HR and people leads saw stress the programme office did not measure.

Outcome: Put employees back in the room at design time. Scaled with the people who made it work, not despite them.
