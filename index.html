Life at Agile Fragile

We are reimagining how teams work — building a new model for people who want real agility without the fragile process. Our community is powered by practitioners who live and breathe better ways of delivering every day.

Our Values

Our values are the foundation of how we work, and guide how we learn, collaborate, and grow together.

Move Forward

We value learning and progress. We solve problems creatively by building on both setbacks and successes.

Face the Hard Things

Even when it is difficult, we address what matters. We give feedback, listen, and have honest dialogue because tough conversations make us better.

Own It

We act as owners in ways big and small. When we are not leading, we empower and trust others. We are accountable for outcomes.

See the Person

Our purpose is in the people. We connect with every person as a person, and take action that has a positive impact on teams and communities.
