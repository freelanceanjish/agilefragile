The proposal.

This is how I think engagement could work if the Human Agile model fits your organization. It is a proposal, not a packaged offering. There is no team behind this site yet. Proposed by Anjish Bhondwe.

The flow

The model starts with people, not frameworks. The Human Agile Index gives an honest read before any rollout conversation.

Human

Start with individuals. Pressure, burnout, who was pushed, who was ignored. The Index surfaces what dashboards hide.

Adapt

Co-design what human agile means in your context. Strip what harms. Keep what helps.

Implement

Teams in the room. Leaders enabling, not tracking. Change built with people, not pushed on a timeline.

Scale

Community, resources, and rhythms that protect humans as agile grows across the organization.

What could be offered

For organizations where agile landed hard on people. The aim is not more ceremony. Remove harm first. These are directions the proposal could take, not services on a shelf.

Restore

Workshops

For teams recovering from Dark Agile. Reset trust before you rebuild.

  • Diagnostic on what is crushing people
  • Strip ceremony theater and dashboard judgment
  • Rebuild psychological safety with leaders in the room

Support

Community

Ongoing peer space for practitioners and leaders mid-transformation.

  • Honest conversation, not performative agile talk
  • Peers who have seen Dark Agile transformations up close
  • Shared language from the Human Agile model

Sustain

Resources

Tools and playbooks that cut admin overhead and keep humans at the center.

  • Lightweight rituals teams can actually keep
  • Leader guides for enablement over tracking
  • Index re-checks as you scale
