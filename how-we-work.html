The flow

Every engagement follows the same human-first arc.

01 Discover

Assess

Human Agile Index, leadership interviews, and an honest read of where agile is breaking people.

02 Align

Adapt

Co-design what human agile means in your context. Strip what harms. Keep what helps.

03 Build

Implement

Teams in the room. Leaders enabling, not tracking. Change built with people, not pushed on a timeline.

04 Sustain

Scale

Community, resources, and rhythms that protect humans as agile grows across the organization.

Three ways we deliver

Restore when harm is done. Support while you change. Sustain when you scale.

Restore

Workshops

For teams recovering from violent agile. Reset trust before you rebuild.

  • Diagnostic on what is crushing people
  • Strip ceremony theater and dashboard judgment
  • Rebuild psychological safety with leaders in the room

Support

Community

Ongoing peer space for practitioners and leaders mid-transformation.

  • Honest conversation, not performative agile talk
  • Peers who have seen violent transformations up close
  • Shared language from the Human Agile model

Sustain

Resources

Tools and playbooks that cut admin overhead and keep humans at the center.

  • Lightweight rituals teams can actually keep
  • Leader guides for enablement over tracking
  • Index re-checks as you scale
