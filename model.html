Human Agile Model

Human is not step one on a slide. Human is the architecture everything else hangs on, for organizations made of people rather than programs or dashboards. Developed by Anjish Bhondwe.

Human
Adapt Implement Scale

Adapt, Implement, and Scale only work when humans are in the room at design time, not notified after the framework is bought.

People first

First on the manifesto. Last in the enterprise rollout.

Human

Name who is hurting. Who is excluded. Encourage before you push. Receiver and deliverer in the room before anything rolls out.

Adapt

Figure out what's actually broken here. Not what the playbook says.

Implement

Build it with the team in the room. Leaders help, they don't hover.

Scale

Grow without losing the people who made it work.

The hard questions

Ask these in your next retro. Watch who gets quiet.

Burnout

Agile vs waterfall

Burnout

  • Why more burnout in agile than waterfall?
  • Waterfall had peaks. Sprints made the peak permanent.
  • Transformation added ceremony on top of the day job

Sprint

Fast or pressured

Sprint

  • Is sprinting delivering fast?
  • Sprints deliver rhythm, not speed
  • Every two weeks a deadline is pressure, not pace

Retro

Learn or track

Retro

  • Is the retrospective for learning?
  • Or a vessel of performance tracking?
  • When leaders mine it for data, the team stops telling the truth

The divide

Dark Agile breaks people to hit dates. Human Agile builds with them to last.

Dark Agile

Learn more

Dark Agile

  • Headcount, not humans
  • Dashboards that judge
  • Blame when it fails
  • Burnout = your problem
  • Ceremony you can't refuse

Learn more

Human Agile

  • Built with people, not rolled out at them
  • In the room when change gets designed
  • Remove harm before adding process
  • Leaders who enable, not track
  • Agile that helps your people

Principles

Encourage

Not push

Encourage

  • Human agile encourages before it pushes
  • Change built with people, not done to them
  • Trust earned, not demanded by timeline

Subtract

Before you add

Subtract

  • Strip theater, surveillance, and harm first
  • Teams can reject ceremony that does not help
  • Less tracking, more enabling
