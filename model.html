Violent Agile

  • Blame the resistance when it fails

  • Ceremony teams cannot reject

Human Agile

  • Built with people from day one
  • Valued in the room when change is designed
  • Subtract harm before adding process
  • Leaders enable teams, not track them
  • Agile that works for people, not against them

Principles

What Human Agile stands on.

Customer and deliverer

Human agile serves the person who receives the product and protects the person who builds it. Both sides of the promise.

Subtract before you add

Strip theater, surveillance, and harm before layering new ceremony.

Built with, not done to

If teams were not in the room when agile was designed, it will fail them.

Sustain as you scale

Human agile is not a launch event. It is how you grow without breaking people.
